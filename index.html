Decision-Support Infrastructure for Invoice Risk, Fraud Detection & AML Signals

RiskLens does not pay invoices and does not issue credit. It is a decision-support infrastructure producing risk scores, fraud signals, and compliance checks. Banks/factoring/funds can finance invoices; RiskLens improves decision quality.

Neden şimdi?

  • Piyasada “naylon fatura”, karşılıksız ticaret ve suistimal örnekleri artıyor.
  • Banka/faktoring/fonlar karar verirken güvenilir risk sinyaline ihtiyaç duyuyor.
  • Tek bir entegrasyonla birçok kuruma lisans satılabilir (SaaS + kullanım bazlı).

Why now?

  • Invoice fraud, fake trade, and abuse patterns are increasing.
  • Banks/factoring/funds need reliable risk signals to finance safely.
  • One integration can be licensed across multiple institutions (SaaS + usage-based).

Business Model (Clear: not “lending/loan-sharking”)

What RiskLens does

  • Invoice analysis + risk scoring
  • Fraud/compliance signals
  • Decision-support report

What RiskLens does NOT do

  • Does not pay invoices
  • Does not lend money
  • Does not distribute capital
This is a software infrastructure and risk intelligence service. A bank/factoring/fund makes the financing decision under its own regulations.

Katılım Bankaları için Özel Değer (İslami şartlara uygun yapı)

  • Faize/ödemeye müdahil olmadan, ticaretin gerçekliğini ve fatura doğruluğunu ölçer.
  • Murabaha/işlem bazlı finansmanlarda “işlem riski”ni azaltır.
  • Uyum ve itibar riski düşer; kurum içi onay süresi hızlanır.

RiskLens büyüdükçe nereye gider?

  • Yeni paneller: AML/KYC risk, tedarik zinciri risk, siber güvenlik sinyalleri
  • Yeni gelir: her yeni modül = ek lisans + kullanım ücreti
  • Global entegrasyon: bankalar + fintech platformları + fonlar

Special Value for Islamic Banks (Sharia-ready structure)

  • Without engaging in interest/payment: measures trade authenticity and invoice integrity.
  • Reduces transaction risk for trade-based finance structures.
  • Lowers compliance/reputation risk and speeds internal approvals.

Where does RiskLens expand?

  • New dashboards: AML/KYC risk, supply-chain risk, cybersecurity signals
  • New revenue: each new module = add-on license + usage fees
  • Global integration: banks + fintech platforms + funds

Dayanıklılık (siber saldırı, internet kesintisi, kriz senaryosu)

  • Bulut + yedekleme + loglama + WAF/Rate limit
  • Offline/queued işlem: bağlantı gelince senkron
  • Yetkilendirme: kurum bazlı erişim, denetim izleri

Resilience (cyber attacks, outages, crisis scenarios)

  • Cloud + backups + logging + WAF/rate limiting
  • Offline/queued operations synced when connectivity returns
  • Institution-based access control & audit trails
